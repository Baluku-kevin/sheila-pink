Welcome to Uganda Martyrs University

Uganda Martyrs University (UMU) is a leading Catholic University in Uganda, committed to academic excellence, holistic human development, and service to society. We offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines. Our mission is to empower individuals to be transformative leaders through quality education, research, and community engagement.
